    • An android app for What.CD and Gazelle

    Project maintained by Gwindow & Twinklebear Hosted on GitHub Pages — Theme by mattgraham

    Description

    An Android app for What.CD, includes everything you love about What.CD as well as barcode scanning for quick music look ups.

    The app will work with a default setup of Gazelle, just change the url to point to your site and enjoy. If you are building from source the WhatAPI project is required.

    Support or Contact

    Have questions? Contact Gwindow or Twinklebear through GitHub, What.CD, or in #whatAndroid.
